About Us

We are The Crown Hotel, a new independent hotel situated in the western edge of Norwich, close to the hospital and research park. Whether you are visiting Norwich for business or leisure, you will find our hotel a comfortable and convenient place to stay. We have 32 rooms available for booking, all with en-suite bathrooms, free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs, and tea and coffee making facilities. You can choose from double or twin rooms, with standard or superior options to suit your budget and preferences. Our hotel also has a restaurant and bar, where you can enjoy a delicious meal or a relaxing drink. We pride ourselves on our friendly and professional service, and we look forward to welcoming you to The Crown Hotel.
